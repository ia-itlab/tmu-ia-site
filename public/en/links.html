Graduation Exhibition 2020

Tokyo Metropolitan University, Hino Campus

Event dates : 2020.02.08 (SAT) - 02.10 (MON) 11:30-17:30

The concept of this year's event, the 11th of its kind, is "bones. Like building bones and adding flesh to them, we have deepened our knowledge in our respective fields of study and acquired expressions and techniques based on the foundation we laid in the first and second years. We hope that visitors will be able to experience the many works that can only be seen in the exhibition, and feel how each of us has fleshed out our work.

Graduation Exhibition 2019

Tokyo Metropolitan University, Hino Campus

Event dates : 2019.02.10 (SUN) - 02.12 (TUE) 10:00-17:00

The concept of the 10th year graduation exhibition of the Department of Industrial Arts, Faculty of System Design, Tokyo Metropolitan University is "Return to the Origin". This culmination of our work is the result of the days we have accumulated since our starting point. Please keep your eyes on our origins, our present, and our continuing future.

Graduation Exhibition 2018

Tokyo Metropolitan University, Hino Campus

Event dates : 2018.02.10 (SAT) - 02.12 (MON) 10:00-17:00

This year's concept is "Quest". We saw learning at the university as a "quest". The continuous space represents our path, and the light at the end of the path represents the answer to our quest. Looking around, the letter Q, the first letter of "Quest," appears. We hope you will take a look at the culmination of the students' efforts to create a landscape and experience that has yet to be seen.

Graduation Exhibition 2017

Tokyo Metropolitan University, Hino Campus

Event dates : 2017.02.04 (SAT) - 02.06 (MON) 10:00-17:00

This year's concept is "Piece". The Industrial Art Course consists of 12 research fields, 6 in Mediate and 6 in Product Design, which are interrelated in a complex way. By combining the various pieces of knowledge and experience, each student will be able to create their own form, which will continue to expand in the future. Please take a look at the works of the students who are taking a new step forward from here.
