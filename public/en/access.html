Access

20 minutes walk from Toyota Station on the JR Chuo Line: Via North Exit.
Or take the Keio bus bound for Hirayama Industrial Park, and get off at Asahigaoka Chuo park (about 5 minutes walk).

From Hachioji Station on the JR Chuo Line: Via North Exit,
Keio Hachioji Station on the Keio Line: Via West Exit,
take the Keio Bus bound for Hino Station.
Or take a bus bound for Toyota Station North Exit, and get off at Owada Sakaue Station (about 10 minutes walk).

15 minutes walk from Kita-Hachioji Station on the JR Hachiko Line.
